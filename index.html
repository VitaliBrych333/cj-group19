Portal about architects

The portal is intended for acquaintance with outstanding figures of architecture from Belarus. Here, you can find information about our compatriots who left a significant mark in the development of the architecture of Belarus. Here are collected their biography with interesting facts, a photo gallery of their work, videos related to their activities or the era in which they lived, and much more.

The portal is designed to promote interest in the architecture of Belarus and the history of their country.
