Architectors list

Baklanov Mikhail Ivanovich

Place of Birth: Ivanovka

Belarusian Soviet architect. Honored Architect of the Byelorussian SSR (1969). born - January 30 (February 12), 1914, village Ivanovka (now Volnovakhsky district, Donetsk region of Ukraine) 1931 - graduated from the school of factory education in Donetsk worked as a draftsman at the local metallurgical plant.

Korol Vladimir Adamovich

Place of Birth: Hegumen

December 14 (27) 1912 - May 28, 1980) - Soviet architect. People's Architect of the USSR (1970). Member of the CPSU (b) since 1940. born 14 (27) December 1912 in the town of Igumen (now Cherven Minsk region of Belarus) [one] . 1931 - graduated from the Vitebsk Art College. Some years worked as a teacher and director of a school in Dzerzhinsk Minsk region. From 1934 to 1941 - he studied at the Faculty of Architecture All-Russian Academy of Arts, successfully defended his thesis the project, and immediately after graduating from the Academy, he was enrolled graduate school.

Longbard Iosif Grigorievich

Place of Birth: Minsk

Belarusian Soviet architect, honored artist Belarusian SSR (1934), winner of the Grand Prix of the World Exhibition in Paris, 1937, Doctor of Architecture (since 1939). Is one of the outstanding architects of Europe of the XX century, whose artistic heritage had a significant impact on development of modern architecture. His architectural work in greatly influenced the formation of the image of the city Minsk and are examples of Belarusian architecture.

Levin Leonid Mendelevich

Place of Birth: Minsk

Deserved Architect of the Republic of Belarus, Laureate of the Lenin Prize and Lenin Komsomol Prize, Volgogradsky Prize Winner Komsomol, twice winner of the State Prize of the Republic Belarus, winner of many prestigious contests and shows Architectural Works, Academician of International and Belarusian Academy of Architecture, Chairman of the Union of Belarus Jewish public associations and communities since 1991 [2] . One of the authors of the memorial complex "Khatyn", for which (in team of authors) was awarded the Lenin Prize.

Parusnikov Mikhail Pavlovich

Place of Birth: Moscow

(1893-1968) - Soviet architect, member of the AA of the USSR (1950), academician of the Academy of Sciences of the BSSR (1950; corresponding member since 1947). Teacher Professor (since 1948). Winner of the State Prize of the Belarusian SSR (1968). 1913 - 1918 studied at the Moscow School of Painting, Sculpture and Architecture

Shabunevsky Stanislav Danilovich

Place of Birth: Skorodnoe

(December 2, 1866/1868, p. Skorodnoe, now the Elsk district of the Gomel region - after 1937, Belomorkanal, GULAG) - Belarusian architect, author a significant part of the building of Gomel beginning of XX century. Honorable citizen of Gomel. Since 1897 S. Shabunevsky served in Gomel at the post urban architect.
